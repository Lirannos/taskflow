About TaskFlow

TaskFlow is a weekly task management web application designed to help you schedule tasks manually or automatically, prioritize them, and synchronize them with your Google Calendar.

Who Built This?

TaskFlow was developed as a project to practice full-stack development using Flask, SQLite, HTML/CSS, and JavaScript, with optional Google Calendar API integration.
